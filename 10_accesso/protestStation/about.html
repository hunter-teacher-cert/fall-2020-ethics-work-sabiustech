WHO ARE WE?>WHAT ARE WE ABOUT?

We are an organization dedicated to the free, safe, and lawful expression of dissent. We are firm believers in the right of all citizens (documented or not) to bring awareness, produce resistance, and otherwise have their voices heard on issues and concerns that are important to their lives, communities, and our nation. We were founded by individuals who saw that the ancient traditions of protest are essential to a vibrant democracy and to enact the kind of reform that brings us ever close to a more just and inclusive nation.


We are dedicated to educating citizens on the legal realms that dictate what they can and cannot do. We provide guidance on how to employ modern technologies to document your activity, but also to connect like-minded reformers all around the counterry...and the world.
